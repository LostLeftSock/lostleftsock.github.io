Team Leadership

Creating amazing design solutions is challenging. I strive to help teams perform their best, all while having fun doing it.

Principles

Support a fun work environment

Solving hard design problems is challenging, but it’s also fun! When everyone can be themselves and bring out their creativity, it leads to better outcomes. If you want smiles on user’s faces, then it starts with the designers creating the experiences.

Be an active participant

To be successful at leading a team, it’s important that you understand the real issues and challenges they face. Knowing the craft well and working alongside them, allows me to push the team in the right direction and offer guidance and mentorship where it counts.

Empower the team

Great teams require the right tools to let them be their most creative selves. This includes access to the best software and hardware for the job. Empowering them also means removing any roadblocks that might pop up and being flexible with how they work best.

Provide actionable feedback

Transparency in communication is critical in making sure the team understands what's needed to be done, what’s working, and what isn’t. I try to deliver honest, practical feedback and jump in to collaborate when they need it.

Listen, inspire, & encourage

Teamwork starts the moment the project does and I involve my teams as early as possible. I actively listen to all ideas, encourage them through challenges, and we celebrate the wins collectively. High-fives are actively encouraged.

Enable learning and growth

Design changes rapidly. Great teams continually evolve and grow, gaining new skills and taking on new roles. I help train team members to grow in their craft and other areas such as presenting, collaborating, and more.
