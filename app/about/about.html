About Me

User Experience, Product, & Design Leader

Hello, I'm James Elder

I've spent more than 20 years creating great experiences by bringing people together. I started my career designing and building websites, became a front-end developer, and moved into design leadership, where I could bring both perspectives together to help teams build better experiences. That journey gave me an appreciation for every part of the product process and a passion for helping product, design, and engineering work better together.

Today, I focus on building and growing high-performing design organizations, bringing structure and clarity to complex product environments while creating the conditions for great experiences to happen. Whether that's facilitating workshops, coaching leaders, refining operating models, or thoughtfully applying AI where it adds real value, my goal is always the same: help people do their best work while creating experiences customers genuinely enjoy.

What I enjoy most isn't shipping a feature. It's seeing a team untangle a difficult problem, rally around a shared vision, and create something that makes life just a little easier for the people using it. Those moments are rarely the result of one great idea. They come from collaboration, curiosity, trust, and a willingness to keep improving.

I've been fortunate to work with startups, consulting organizations, and global enterprises across a wide variety of industries. Every company is different, but one lesson has remained the same: the best experiences come from asking good questions, listening carefully, and keeping people at the center of every decision.

Outside of work, I'm endlessly curious. I enjoy exploring new technology, experimenting with AI, building the occasional side project, and finding better ways to solve everyday problems. I also believe work should be fun. The best teams I've been part of worked hard, challenged each other, celebrated wins together, and never underestimated the value of a well-timed high-five.

What I Focus On

Creating Great Experiences

Every decision starts with the people using the product. Great experiences come from understanding their needs, simplifying complexity, and designing with intention.

Building Great Teams

The best products are built by people who trust each other, collaborate openly, and enjoy solving difficult problems together. My role is to create the environment where they can do their best work.

Bringing Clarity to Complexity

Whether it's refining an operating model, aligning product and engineering, or thoughtfully applying AI, I enjoy bringing structure to complex challenges so teams can move forward with confidence.
